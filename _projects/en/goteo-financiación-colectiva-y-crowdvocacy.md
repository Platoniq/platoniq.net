# What have we accomplished so far?

{% metrics { "items": [{"text":"Money Raised","number":"21M"},{"text":"Successful Projects","number":"83,3%"},{"text":"Community","number":"355k"}] } %}



* Goteo is a project incubator that develops and connects good ideas with the contexts that need them;
* an online social network that enables micro-donations from the public, institutions, and companies to innovative projects of their choice: an alternative funding channel in line with new digital models;
* an efficient, open, and productive platform capable of connecting initiatives, creators, and entrepreneurs with people in their community: a meeting place for citizen participation in future social and cultural agendas;
* a crowdvocacy tool that drives the transformation of public institutions by promoting citizen advocacy.

Our data speaks for itself: more than 21 million euros redistributed among the 2,022 projects funded to date, and 350,000 people working together to make it happen. With an 84% success rate. Yes, 84%. 

At Goteo, we don’t just offer funding—we’re a 
team of people who support projects and guide them every step of the way. **We facilitate partnerships and build a community around each campaign.**

We are **[transparent](https://stats.goteo.org/home/es)**, **rigorous** (of the 9,796 projects received, 2,654 were ultimately published), and **committed to the Social and Solidarity Economy and social transformation.**
